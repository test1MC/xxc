Volkswagen

 

Volkswagen, or VW for short, is a car company from Germany. The word volkswagen means “People’s car” in German. Its headquarters are in Wolfsburg, Lower Saxony. It was started in the 1930s, at the request of the country’s leader, Adolf Hitler, to produce a car designed by Ferdinand Porsche. 5.

 

As of 2014, Volkswagen's current slogan is "Das Auto," which means "The Car" in German. The car company launched this new slogan in 2007 at the Frankfurt Motor Show.
