The history of the BMW slogan “Sheer Driving Pleasure”

The BMW slogan “Sheer Driving Pleasure” has evolved over the years from various brand claims in German. The term “pleasure” first appeared in the 1930s in BMW ads.

 

In 2006 after BMW decided to stop running its Ultimate Driving Machine campaign they launched "Joy Is BMW". This new campaign was focused on safety and fuel efficiency.
