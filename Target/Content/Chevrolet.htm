Chevrolet

The Chevrolet emblem has yellow, gold, silver and black tints. All these colors mean the company’s rich heritage, ingenuity, grace, and passion. The Chevrolet emblem has a custom typeface. The make Chevrolet does not lose positions in the market.

 

History Behind the Chevrolet Name Chevrolet got its name from famed Buick race car driver Louis Chevrolet. Louis joined forces with William Durant -- founder of General Motors -- to built the first Chevrolet Motor Company vehicle in 1911.

 

GMC does not own Chevy and neither does Chevy own GMC. Both of these brands are owned by GM, that is, General Motors, which makes them different brands owned by the same company. So, GMC and Chevy are not completely similar and hence have some differences in design and construction.
